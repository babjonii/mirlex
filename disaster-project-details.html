Construction Of National Disaster Management Centre At Dodoma Tanzania (Phase 1)

Here's how Mirlex Engineering Works Ltd contributed to the electrification of the National Disaster Management Centre at Dodoma, Tanzania (Phase 1):

Initial Assessment and Planning: Mirlex Engineering Works Ltd conducted a thorough assessment and collaborated closely with project stakeholders to plan the electrical infrastructure for the National Disaster Management Centre.

Customized Electrical Design: Our team developed a tailored electrical design to meet the specific requirements of the National Disaster Management Centre, ensuring optimal functionality and safety.

Procurement and Preparation: Mirlex Engineering Works Ltd procured high-quality electrical equipment and prepared the site for seamless installation and integration.

Equipment Installation and Integration: Skilled technicians from Mirlex Engineering Works Ltd installed and integrated the electrical systems, including power distribution units, lighting, and safety devices, to ensure the efficient operation of the National Disaster Management Centre.

Commissioning and Submission: Following the completion of installation, the electrical systems underwent rigorous testing and commissioning procedures to ensure compliance with safety standards and regulatory requirements. Once commissioned, the project was submitted for official use.

Through our expertise and dedication to quality, Mirlex Engineering Works Ltd successfully contributed to the electrification of the National Disaster Management Centre at Dodoma, Tanzania (Phase 1), enhancing its operational efficiency and resilience in times of crisis.
