Our Story

Mirlex Engineering Works Limited began with a vision to revolutionize the electrical services industry in Tanzania. Founded by a team of dedicated professionals led by Eng. Alex R. Lyimo & Mr. Miraji F. Msuya, our journey began with a commitment to excellence and innovation.

From humble beginnings, we steadily built our reputation by delivering exceptional quality and reliability in every project we undertook. Our focus on customer satisfaction and dedication to exceeding expectations propelled us forward, earning the trust of clients across various sectors.

Driven by our passion for engineering and a desire to make a meaningful impact, we expanded our services to encompass a comprehensive range of electrical and mechanical solutions. From electrifying new buildings to implementing cutting-edge technologies in renewable energy and disaster management, our portfolio continues to evolve to meet the ever-changing needs of our clients.

As we continue to grow and expand our horizons, our core values remain at the heart of everything we do, guiding us towards a future of continued success and positive transformation.
